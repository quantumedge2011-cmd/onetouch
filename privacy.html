Sovereign Privacy Manifesto

I. Local-First Architecture

Your data never leaves your device unencrypted. All records are stored in a local browser database (IndexedDB). We believe that your digital footprint should be as private as your physical one.

II. End-to-End Encryption

We use the high-security Web Crypto API (AES-GCM) to encrypt your local database. Keys are derived dynamically from your authenticated Google Identity and are never transmitted to or stored on any server.

III. Private Cloud Sync

Synchronization occurs exclusively with your hidden Google Drive appDataFolder. This is a sanctuary within the cloud, inaccessible to other apps and invisible to the standard Drive UI.

IV. Local AI Inference

The Oracle (WebLLM) runs models directly on your hardware via WebGPU. Your whispers to the AI stay within the local circuits of your machine, ensuring total conversational sovereignty.

V. AdSense & Cookie Compliance

To support this project, we utilize Google AdSense. Google serves advertisements on this site based on your visits here and across the web. To ensure transparency, we adhere to the following protocols:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on your site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to your sites and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.
  • Third-party vendors and ad networks may also use cookies to track user interactions for advertising purposes.
